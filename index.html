Digital circuits

This project involved the creation of a functional traffic light system, comprising three states: red for stop, green for go, and orange for preparing to stop. The complexity of the circuit design required the utilization of down counters and multiple comparators. Despite the challenges, the project was successfully implemented and achieved the desired functionality.
This project involved the development of a digital clock utilizing logic gates, along with an alarm function that triggers every ten minutes. The implementation required the integration of six counters: two for tracking seconds, two for minutes, and two for hours. Additionally, comparators were employed to monitor the condition every 10 minutes and activate the alarm signal. The successful completion of this project showcases the effective utilization of logic gates and counters to create a reliable and functional digital clock with an alarm feature.

Channel coding

This project served as a significant milestone in my master's thesis research. It focused on developing a relay system to establish a virtual Multiple-Input Multiple-Output (MIMO) system using coded cooperation. The primary objective was to enhance the Bit Error Rate (BER) performance of our communication systems. To achieve this, the relay system was integrated with LDPC codes for encoding and decoding. The successful combination of the relay system and LDPC codes showcased promising results, demonstrating the potential for improved communication performance in practical applications.
This phase of the project involved a significant improvement over the initial research point by replacing the relay systems with the Reflective Intelligent Surface (RIS) architecture. Through extensive MATLAB simulations, it was observed that the performance results achieved with the RIS architecture surpassed those obtained with the relay system. The superior performance of the RIS architecture validated its potential as a more effective solution for enhancing communication systems. These findings underscored the significance of incorporating RIS technology in improving overall system performance and paved the way for further advancements in the field.
